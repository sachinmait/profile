Profile Narrative

Prof. (Dr.) Sachin Gupta is a distinguished academician and institutional administrator, currently serving as the Dean of Research and Innovation and Professor of Computer Science at Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology (MAIT). His professional legacy spans over 24 years of elite research, dynamic teaching, and institution building.

Over his post-Ph.D. tenure of more than 9 years, his research on Wireless Sensor Networks, Blockchain systems, and Artificial Intelligence has culminated in the supervision of 10 Ph.D. scholars, 5 patents, and the authorship of essential textbooks implemented across CBSE's nationwide higher-secondary curricula for Grades 9 through 12.
